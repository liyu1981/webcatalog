What is WebCatalog?

Mac apps are great. You can launch them quickly from your dock, resize the windows freely and are protected against web trackers. But sometimes, your favorite services don't have official Mac apps. Instead, you can only access these services via their websites.

WebCatalog is a free app which lets you turn any websites into native-like Mac apps (also called site-specific browsers). Now, with WebCatalog, you can launch your favorite apps quickly in self-contained, distraction-free windows. Each app's data is stored separately, protecting you from cross-website trackers and preserving your privacy. Additionally, WebCatalog lets you customize them with workspaces, dark mode, and more.

Notable Features

Catalog - Install apps quickly from our curated catalog.

AppView - Run any website like a real Mac app.

Workspace - Use and switch between multiple accounts.

Customization - Customize everything to fit your taste.
